About KFC

KFC (Kentucky Fried Chicken) has been serving up its iconic fried chicken since 1952. Founded by Colonel Harland Sanders, KFC is now one of the largest fast-food chains in the world, known for its original recipe, crispy chicken, and Southern-style comfort food.
